At home the rabbits hiss a salad.

chisels chip at a thick curd,

My sisters stick, my bowl staggers,

willows shell me to the ground

At the milk pan deer are crowbars,

at the milk pan my melon sobs.

My child has seen me through the willows,

yet I never planted him...

My son has hissed me from the salad..

Apple twigs squirt porcupines

Rabbits at the milk pan, Daughter

rabbits shake the blankets down.
